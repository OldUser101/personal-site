# About Me

I'm a hobbyist programmer in the UK, particuarly interested in systems
programming. I'm a contributor to a few open-source projects and have a
few of my own. Here's a couple of those:

- [nlock](https://github.com/OldUser101/nlock), a minimalist screen locker
    for Wayland, based around a theme I found cool. Aims to be very compatible,
    supporting BSD targets as well as Linux.

- [tars](https://git.ngill.net/cgit/tars.git), a template-based static site
    generator that power this website. I thought it would be nice to implement
    my own rather than relying on an existing one.

- tetra, an experimental, minimal, Linux distribution I'm working on in the
    background when I get time. It's more of a collection of smaller projects,
    of which [lvinit](https://git.ngill.net/cgit/tetra/lvinit.git) is the most
    "complete" so far.

Take a look at my [now page](/now/index.html) for other things I'm currently
working on/doing.

My favourite programming language is probably Rust, but I quite enjoy writing
C, C++, and Python too, depending on the situation.
